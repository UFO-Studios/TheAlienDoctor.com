About TheAlienDoctor

Hello humans, I am TheAlienDoctor but you can call me Alien and welcome to my website!

I've been playing Minecraft for over 10 years, and creating Minecraft content for just under half of that! I am most interested in Minecraft for it's technical aspects, such as making farms and redstone contraptions. However I also enjoy out aspects of the game such as building and PvP, even if I'm not the best at them 😅. On this channel you can find my long term let's play series on the Truly Bedrock SMP, as well as tutorials and other informative videos about Minecraft Bedrock edition!

One of my aims with this channel is to create an amazing community, which I have named The Alien Empire! To join, simply subscribe! And maybe consider joining the Discord too ;)

f you're interested in Minecraft Bedrock edition or just looking for some Minecraft fun, you've come to the right place!

FAQ:

Why do you play Bedrock? Because I enjoy it more than playing Java edition. Also, most of the big Minecraft content creators play on Java - I want to change that!

How can I play with you? You can apply to join UFO SMP on my Discord, once you are over the chat level of 5. UFO SMP is my community cross play server, running Java server software with a plugin allowing Bedrock players to join as well.

Any more questions? Join my Discord and ask me there :)
