Description

This pack stops you from being able to enter The End in survival mode.

To do this, it makes the Eye of Enders impossible to craft, as well as removing any End Portal Tiles in the world (not the frames, just the tiles). Because of this, it also stops you from being able to craft any Ender Chests.
