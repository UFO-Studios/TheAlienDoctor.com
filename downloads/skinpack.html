Description

This is a custom skin pack for Minecraft Bedrock edition that adds in many of Aliens skins used in his videos.

  • Please note: due to the bug MCPE-152216 in the latest Minecraft version, you cannot actually equip custom skins. There are 2 ways you can go around this, firstly you can use a version switcher (I use FoxyNoTails, but there are easier ones to setup, such as BedrockLauncher) and enable the skin you want to use in an older version. Alternatively you can rename the pack to a .zip and enable each skin individually as a custom skin.
