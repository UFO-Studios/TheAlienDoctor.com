Description

This pack adds in a slider that enabled you to change when fog scales with your render distance or not. Currently you can remove Nether, Water and Lava fog.

Instructions

Once you have installed this pack, apply it to your global resources. Once applied, click on it and you should see a cog wheel. Clicking on this will open a slider menu that allows you to change which fog is hidden.
