Description

A simple addon that adds Hardcore into Minecraft Bedrock edition. To combat cheating, it will automatically put you back in survival if you go into creative, as well as change your world settings. NOTE: Unfortunately its not possible to stop commands from being ran entirely. It also adds in the Hardcore hearts from Java edition.
