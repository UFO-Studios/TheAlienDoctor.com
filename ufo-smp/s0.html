Season 0 info

  • This season is going be be community based, so you are encouraged to build s possibly smaller scale base than you would normally on the main mooshroom island. There is also a shopping district but it is also encouraged that you allow your farms to be open to the public.
  • Players that have a "." at the start of there username mean they are playing on bedrock edition (unless they are a java player that chose to have a dot at the start of there name)
  • The server runs on Paper, this means it uses Java edition technical mechanics, even if you are playing on Bedrock.
  • You can do "/grave" to teleport back to any of your unlooted graves.
  • Graves can be opened by anyone after 30 mins, and after an hour the grave will despawn.
